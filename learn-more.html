About Horizon Dynamics International

Innovating for a safer, smarter tomorrow.

Our History

Founded in 1968 by visionary computer scientist, Russell Maxwell, Horizon Defense Industries (HDI) began as a foundational neural network firm. While Maxwell's initial focus was on early artificial intelligence models, the company quickly expanded into defense technologies.

Over the decades, HDI has pioneered advancements in bio-intelligent neural interfaces, setting the standard for security and public sector infrastructure. Today, HDI continues to lead the industry through strategic acquisitions and transformative technological research.

Leadership

Olivia Bennett

Executive Director

Olivia Bennett brings over 30 years of experience in high-level strategic planning and defense infrastructure to HDI. Prior to joining Horizon, Ms. Bennett served in a senior capacity for the Department of Defense (DOD), where she led initiatives on national security communication protocols.
