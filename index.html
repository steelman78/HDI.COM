Our History

Horizon Dynamics International was founded in 1968 by Russell Maxwell, an engineer and systems theorist who believed that the future of national security would be defined not by singular inventions, but by integrated technological ecosystems.

Maxwell’s founding vision was clear: develop technologies that would anticipate emerging threats rather than merely respond to them. Today, HDI remains guided by his enduring principle: the horizon is not a limit — it is a responsibility.

Strategic Partnerships

Federal Agencies

HDI collaborates with federal agencies to design and maintain mission-critical systems that support national resilience and public safety through advanced data analytics.
